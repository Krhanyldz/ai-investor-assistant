# Legal Review Checklist

This checklist tracks items that must be resolved before AI Investor Assistant is used in production. The current disclosure pages are pre-production drafts and are not legally complete.

- Confirm the controller's legal identity, registered address, and private user contact channel.
- Review GDPR Article 13 notice requirements, applicable legal bases, and any consent-withdrawal requirements.
- Identify all processors and subprocessors, and complete required DPAs.
- Confirm hosting locations, international transfers, safeguards, and transfer impact requirements.
- Define retention, deletion, backup, and account-closure schedules.
- Review cookies, Supabase auth session handling, and signed-out localStorage consent fallback.
- Complete investment-advice, brokerage, suitability, fiduciary, and financial-promotion regulatory review.
- Review AI disclosure language, human-verification requirements, and prohibited presentation of AI output as trade instructions.
- Obtain final lawyer approval before production launch.
